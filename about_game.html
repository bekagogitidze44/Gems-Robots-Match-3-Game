Gems & Robots: About Game

Game Modes:

1. Score Challenge:

Embark on a thrilling journey to accumulate a specific number of points. Each distinct shape on the grid contributes varying scores. Strategize your moves to reach the target score and conquer this challenging puzzle adventure. Unleash your matching skills and explore the diverse point values hidden within the vibrant game board.

2. Bot Duel:

Engage in a dynamic battle against an AI opponent in this intense Bot Duel mode. The starting move is randomized, adding an element of surprise to each match. Compete against the bot to be the first to achieve the required score. With only 30 seconds per turn, quick thinking and strategic decision-making become key. Will you outsmart the bot and emerge victorious in this fast-paced competition? Test your reflexes and match-three prowess in a race against time.

3. Time Crunch:

Immerse yourself in the Time Crunch mode, where every move matters. Race against the clock to reach your objective before time runs out. With a limited timeframe for each move, the pressure is on to make swift and effective decisions. Navigate through the puzzle, strategically swapping tiles to maximize your score within the given time constraints. Can you handle the time crunch and emerge as the ultimate puzzle master?

4. Combo Frenzy:

Enter the world of Combo Frenzy, a mode that rewards strategic combos. Explore the synergy between different moves to unleash powerful combinations. Earn bonus points for consecutive successful moves and master the art of chain reactions. Dive into the puzzle, experiment with various combinations, and witness the excitement of creating cascading combos. Can you string together the perfect sequence and achieve the highest score in this thrilling puzzle challenge?
